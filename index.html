The Artist

About Me

I'm Gokul Krishna M B — an artist shaped by the quiet beginnings of the pandemic. Starting with realism and evolving into hyper-realism, I found my true canvas in human skin.

I create bespoke tattoos that turn personal stories into lasting works of art, combining clean linework, depth, and precision. Each piece is thoughtfully designed to reflect individuality and meaning.

Trained with a high end course in Tattooing and Piercing from Aliens Tattoo, India, I focus on delivering tattoos that are not just visually striking, but deeply personal.
